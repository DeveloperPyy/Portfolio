03 : About me

My Journey

I started my journey in engineering with a fascination for how things work and a passion for robotics. During my time in mechanical engineering, I discovered my love for automation and computer vision. This led me to specialize in building intelligent systems that merge software and hardware seamlessly.

What I Bring to the Table

Today, as a Robotics Engineer, I focus on designing and implementing cutting-edge solutions in robotics and computer vision. From creating algorithms for autonomous systems to enhancing object detection capabilities, I strive to deliver innovative and impactful results that bridge the gap between technology and real-world applications.

What Drives Me Forward

I’m driven by a deep curiosity to explore the potential of robotics in transforming industries. Beyond work, I enjoy gaming, leather crafting, 3D printing, and experimenting with new technologies. My goal is to contribute to innovative solutions that make life smarter, simpler, and more connected.
